How to play

The game pulls a random Wikipedia article and displays it for a short amount of time. After that time, the words are blanked out. The goal of the game is to fill the blanks correctly!

Try to clear out as many levels as you can — as you progress, more words are blanked out.

Feeling overwhelmed? You can restart from Level 1.
